The "Nigerian Prince" Email

👑📧
  • Absurd story, bad grammar, huge fortune promise
  • We laugh → "Who would fall for this?"
  • Reality: it filters out only the vulnerable

From Princes to CEOs

Case: Business Email Compromise (BEC)

  • Spoofed CEO email
  • Urgent wire transfer request
  • Result: hundreds of thousands lost

Hacking the Human Mind

🧠
  • Authority bias: trust in leaders
  • Urgency effect: shortcuts under pressure
  • Default trust: humans assume honesty

A “Stupid Scam” Is Never Stupid

🔍📈
  • Outlandish scams filter for easy victims
  • Sophisticated psychology at play
  • Not about gullibility, but human nature

Building Smarter Trust

🛡️✅
  • Pause before acting on urgency
  • Check sender identity (domain, reply-to)
  • Normalize double-checking authority
